UNLOCK BOOTLOADER

1. adb reboot bootloader
2. adb device / devices
3. fastboot oem unlock / unlock-go 
4. fastboot flash oem unlock
5. fastboot flshing unlock-go 
6. fastboot oem device-info
7. fastboot unlock devices-status
8. adb / fastboot reboot


TWRP & ROOT 

1. adb reboot bootloader
2. adb device / devices
3. fastboot oem / unlock device-info
4. fastboot flash recovery twrpc2.img
5. fastboot flashing recovery twrp 
6. adb reboot recovery
